Huynh Ngoc Duy

I'm Duy, a 19-year-old Vietnamese guy. I was born and grew up in a beautiful beach city: Da Nang. I had lived in Da Nang city for 12 years before moving to Ho Chi Minh City. I am a mixture of the calmness of Danang and the excitement of Saigon (a.k.a Ho Chi Minh city).

I don't have any background in programming before going to college; hence I still on the way to explore what I am interested in and want to pursue in the long run. Currently, I want to become a software engineering. I am focusing on learning and working on web development and mobile development.
